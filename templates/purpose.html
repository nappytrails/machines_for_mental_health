Mental Health Importance



Our purpose is to identify employees battling mental illnesses. According to this survey many people struggle in their everyday life to overcome such challenges which can have an impact on their physical health, social and occupational life. We used the survey below to prevent such damages to people and to the economy of the companies. Based on this data we were able to deploy a test which people can take to find out if they need to seek traatment for their mental health or not. The purpose of this quiz is to prevent the damage that mental health can have on the physical health of the people and those around them.
